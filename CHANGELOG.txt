Phantom 0.1
-----------

[*] Initial release

Features:

- N preserving policy on the individual clouds of FutureGrid.
- N can change throughout the lifetime of the domain.
- Simple web application for creating, adjusting, and termination domains
- Implements a subset of the 'AWS Autoscaling Groups' protocol.

- EPU services are backed by zookeeper
- Authentication and web application data is backed by MySQL
